Our Mission

Rotaract Club Jabali is part of a global network of young adults passionate about service, leadership, and professional growth. Our mission is to create a platform where individuals aged 18 and above can connect, serve, and grow while addressing critical issues in our communities.

We aim to nurture leaders who take bold steps in solving real-world problems through collective action, mentorship, and sustainable projects. Rooted in the Rotary motto “Service Above Self,” we champion integrity, diversity, and positive impact.

Strategic Goals

1. Empowering Youth Leadership

We provide training, mentorship, and leadership opportunities for our members to become ethical, confident, and innovative leaders across all sectors.

2. Driving Sustainable Community Service

Our club identifies pressing social, environmental, and economic issues and responds through impactful service projects designed with long-term sustainability in mind.

3. Building Global Connections

Through collaboration with other Rotaract and Rotary clubs worldwide, we engage in cultural exchange, joint initiatives, and networking opportunities that broaden our global perspective.

4. Promoting Professional Development

We host career-focused events, workshops, and panel discussions to help members develop soft and technical skills that align with their career aspirations.

Leadership Structure

The club operates with a strong governance model that encourages transparency, collaboration, and mentorship. Each role within the board has clear responsibilities, ensuring effective service delivery and team growth.

  • President: Provides overall leadership and represents the club at official functions.
  • Vice President: Assists the president and leads when the president is unavailable.
  • Secretary: Maintains all club records, meeting minutes, and communications.
  • Treasurer: Manages the club’s finances and prepares budget reports.
  • Directors: Oversee functional areas such as:
    • Membership & Recruitment
    • Community Service
    • Professional Development
    • Public Relations & Media
    • Environment & Sustainability

This structure fosters accountability and helps members develop leadership in a practical setting.

Key Projects and Initiatives

Medical Camps and Health Awareness

We regularly partner with local health professionals to offer free checkups, screenings, and health education in underserved communities. Past camps have included blood pressure testing, HIV awareness, and mental health sensitization.

Mentorship and Career Talks

We invite industry professionals, alumni, and entrepreneurs to mentor students and early-career members. Topics range from CV writing and personal branding to tech careers and financial literacy.

Environmental Sustainability

In our “Green Jabali” initiative, we conduct tree-planting drives, clean-up activities, and waste management awareness events to promote environmental conservation and climate action.

Rotaract For Schools

This outreach program targets high school students to build self-esteem, leadership skills, and academic confidence through interactive workshops and motivational talks.

How to Join Rotaract

Rotaract is open to everyone aged 18 and above who is interested in leadership, service, and growth. Whether you're a student, entrepreneur, or professional, there's a place for you here.

Steps to Join:

  1. Attend an open meeting or service event (check our social media for dates).
  2. Submit a short registration form (available at meetings).
  3. Engage in a short orientation and mentorship session.
  4. Receive your membership badge and start making impact!

We meet every Saturday from 2:00 PM at Santuri Salon, The Mall, Nairobi. You can also reach out via email or social media to get started.

Did You Know?

Rotaract was founded in 1968 and is one of Rotary International’s most successful youth programs, with over 200,000 members globally. It became an official membership type of Rotary in 2019—meaning Rotaractors can now serve on equal footing with Rotarians worldwide.
